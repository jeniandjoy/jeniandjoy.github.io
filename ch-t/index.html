Jeni & Joy

Jeni & Joy was founded by sisters Jenny and Joyce from Calgary, Alberta, Canada . Our skincare products are locally handcrafted in small batches using the freshest ingredients purchased from Canadian retailers. We are passionate about skincare and would love to share our creations with you!

Made in Small Batches

We produce each product with fresh ingredients and in small batches so we can closely monitor the quality. Fresh ingredients means the product will last longer and give your skin the greatest benefits!

Hand Picked Ingredients

Each ingredient is hand picked and is among our personal favorites! We only chose ingredients we felt would be the best for your skin. Our products are packed with many vitamins and plant sourced ingredients that are able to effectively nourish and protect your skin from external elements. All of our products are paraben-free and made with safe and non-comedogenic ingredients.

Try Today!

We encourage you to try our products to ensure that it is suitable for you. We would love to hear your feedback and comments as we are constantly looking for ways to improve our products. Request a sample and share your thoughts with us!
