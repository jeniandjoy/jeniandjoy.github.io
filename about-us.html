About Us

Jeni & Joy was founded in 2016 by sisters Jenny Tse and Joyce Tse. We were born in Hong Kong and were raised in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. We are both dreamers, avid World travelers, animal lovers, foodies and music enthusiasts. We pretty much do everything together!

We compliment each other in our flaws and strengths. It's hard to imagine a life without each other.


Hi, I'm Joyce, the older of the two sisters. I studied at Queen's University graduate and have been working as an Engineer for the past few years.

Learning about skincare formulation allowed me to merge my love for Science and Skincare. I started making my own skincare products as a fun side hobby, and soon realized other people could benefit from my products too! I wanted to be able to have complete control over what I put on my face and be able to understand each ingredient in my products inside out.

We appreciate any feedback or suggestions as we want to constantly improve our products! I hope you love our products as much as I do!


Hello! I'm the younger sister, Jenny. I'm a graduate from the University of Calgary and am currently an Accountant.

I've always been found DIY projects and crafts intriguing, so I end up making a lot of things by myself. Having struggled with problem skin (dryness, sensitivity, acne, pigmentation) for many years, I immediately grew interest in making my own skincare when I came across a tutorial for a vitamin C serum, and that propelled me to research in depth about skincare.

After using our own DIY products I saw great improvement in my skin within weeks, and I was hooked! I don't think I've ever found anything that worked as well!
