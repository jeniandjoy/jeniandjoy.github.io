Our Philosophy

Jeni and Joy is committed to bringing you the best products for your skin. We are dedicated to showing our customers the simplicity of effective skincare by providing products made with top quality ingredients. Our products are handmade with love from Canada.

Simple Low pH Skincare

The basics of good skin starts with moisture, sun protection, mild exfoliation and low pH. A short and concise ingredient list is all you really need to achieve this. Scientific studies have proven that the average person has a pH of 4.7 on the outer layer of their skin. This pH barrier can easily be disrupted by any products over the pH of 4.7, including water! If the pH is out of balance, it takes anywhere from 3-8 hours for the skin to return to its normal pH. Maintaining the skin at a slightly acidic pH of around 4.7 will help protect it from the elements and bacteria. All of our products are created to have a pH of 4.7, with the exception of products intended for exfoliation, which will have an even lower pH.



Ingredient Transparency

Most individuals are unaware of the ingredients which go into their skincare products. What is its purpose in the product, and is it truly safe for the skin? We strive to answer these questions and use only ingredients best for you. Each ingredient used in our products serves a purpose and we encourage all our customers to learn about each ingredient on our Products page to understand what it’s made of.



Top Quality Ingredients

Our products only contain quality ingredients, carefully chosen to ensure they are beneficial for the skin and in well balanced quantities. We resolve not to use low cost filler ingredients in order to pack the most benefit in each drop of product.


The definition of a natural product is loosely regulated, so the majority of products you see labeled “natural” still will contain synthetic ingredients. Just because a product is natural doesn’t mean it’s good for you! For example, chili pepper oil is natural, but it wouldn’t be recommended for skin application. “Naturally derived” is another term that frequently misleads consumers. Naturally derived ingredients are often altered so much that they bear little resemblance to their original natural state.


We prefer to choose ingredients for our products based on its properties and benefits instead of whether it’s natural or synthetic. Most of our products are based primarily on natural plant oils due to their rich benefits. We choose ingredients which are non-comedogenic and are safe for sensitive skin.
