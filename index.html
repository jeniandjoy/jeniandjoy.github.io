Jeni & Joy

Jeni & Joy was founded by sisters Jenny and Joyce from Calgary, Alberta, Canada . Our skincare products are locally handcrafted in small batches with the finest ingredients. We are passionate about skincare and would love to share our creations with you!

Made in Small Batches

Because our products are made in small batches, you can ensure that your products are fresh when you receive them.

Hand Picked Ingredients

We have hand picked the ingredients we felt would be most safe and beneficial for our skin and yours. Our products are packed with many plant derived ingredients and vitamins that are able to effectively nourish your skin and protect it from external elements. All of our products are free of parabens and are made with non-comedogenic ingredients.

Try Today!

Because everyone's skin is different, we encourage you to try our products first before buying to ensure our product is suitable for you. We would love to hear your feedback and comments as we are constantly looking for ways to improve our products. Request a sample and let us know what you think!
