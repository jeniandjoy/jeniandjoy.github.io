Products

Jeni and Joy is committed to bringing you the best products for your skin. We are dedicated to showing our customers the simplicity of effective skincare by providing products made with top quality ingredients. Our products are handmade with love from Canada.

Quality

No low cost filler ingredients, only ingredients that matter.

Simple

We believe good skincare is simple. The basics of good skin starts with moisture, sun protection, mild exfoliation and low pH. Skincare doesn't need to be complicated!

Transparent

You deserve to know what's in your skincare. We strive to help you fully understand what each ingredient is and what it does for you.
